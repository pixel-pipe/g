EXPLANATION

Goal

Reach the flag at the end of each stage. Stage 3 has a boss near the flag, and Stage 4 is an aerial route built around paired seesaw platforms, mushroom relay perches, and rideable side-view airplanes.

Controls

  • Move: A / D or Arrow Left / Arrow Right
  • Jump: W, Arrow Up, or Space
  • Double jump: press jump again in the air
  • Firebolt while Super Mario: Circle button, X key, or F key
  • Enter pipe: S or Arrow Down while standing on a pipe
  • Return to main menu: R
  • PlayStation controller: left stick or D-pad to move, Cross to jump, Options/R1/R2 to return to menu

Items

Question blocks can release 1UP, grow, shrink, flight, and helper companion items. Rare blocks and hidden bricks can contain stronger items.
